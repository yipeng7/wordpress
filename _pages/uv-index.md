                    <h1 style="font-family:Arial;color:black;font-weight:900;font-size:50px">UV Index 3 - 5</h1>
                    &nbsp;
                    <h4><strong>Danger Category&nbsp; :&nbsp;</strong>&nbsp;<strong>MODERATE</strong></h4>
                    <strong>Sunburn Time&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;:</strong> Approximately 30 Minutes
                    <strong>Must Haves&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;:</strong> Sunscreen SPF 30-50+ (waterproof, broad sprectrum)

                    <strong>Must Haves&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;:</strong> Sunscreen (waterproof, broad spectrum) SPF 100+), Sun protective hat UPF 50+, UV protection sun glasses EPF 9
